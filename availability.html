Fast Request Format

Send the details that matter.

Use this checklist when calling, texting, or emailing. The more complete the request, the faster the farm can confirm what is ready.

Plant namesExample: Podocarpus, Seagrape, Areca Palm, Green Buttonwood.
Size and quantityExample: 25G, 45G, field-grown, number of plants, or target height.
Project locationCity, county, access notes, and delivery distance.
Buyer typeContractor, builder, municipality, estate manager, landscaper, or homeowner.
Pickup or delivery needsLet the farm know whether you need pickup, delivery coordination, or a farm walk.
TimingBid deadline, install window, or date the material needs to be ready.

Photos, plant schedules, and bid sheets can be sent by text or email.
