The Farm

A working grower, not a showroom.

Abel Tree Farm is a working grower in Loxahatchee Groves, west of Palm Beach. The farm helps buyers find palms, trees, and hedging that fit South Florida conditions, project timing, and available sizes.

Contractors need material that is healthy, ready, and sized correctly. Homeowners need straight answers before spending serious money. A short call helps confirm what is available and whether a farm visit makes sense.

Help Us Answer Fast

Have the right details ready.

Whether you are a landscape contractor bidding a job or a homeowner upgrading your property, these details help the farm confirm availability faster.

  • Plant speciesExample: Podocarpus, Seagrape, Areca Palm, Green Buttonwood.
  • Desired size and quantityContainer size, field-grown needs, target height, and number of plants.
  • Project cityCity, county, delivery distance, and access notes if relevant.
  • Timeline or deadlineBid date, install window, pickup timing, or delivery needs.

Delivery coordination
