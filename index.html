The Farm

A working grower for South Florida project buyers.

Abel Tree Farm is a working wholesale grower in Loxahatchee Groves, west of Palm Beach. The farm helps contractors, builders, municipalities, estate managers, property managers, and large-project buyers source palms, trees, hedging, and field-grown material that fit South Florida conditions, project timing, and available sizes.

Call before visiting so the farm can confirm what is ready, what quantities are realistic, and whether a farm walk makes sense for the job.

How Buying Works

Send the list. Confirm availability. Walk the farm when needed.

The process is built around current sizes, realistic quantities, timing, and planned farm visits for larger orders or specimen selections.

1

Send the plant list

Call or text the material, size, quantity, project city, and timeline.

2

Confirm availability and fit

Review current sizes, quantities, substitutions, and timing.

3

Schedule a farm walk if needed

Walk the rows for larger orders, specimen material, or selections that need to be seen before approval.

4

Coordinate next steps

Confirm pickup, loading needs, delivery coordination, access requirements, and project timing.

Project Support

Selection, farm walks, pickup, and delivery coordination.

Abel Tree Farm helps wholesale and large-project buyers coordinate the practical details that matter once plant material is selected.

01

Delivery coordination

Coordinate quantities, access requirements, and timing to help keep projects on schedule.

02

Site-matching guidance

Discuss salt tolerance, wind exposure, sun, water, privacy goals, and mature sizing before choosing material.

03

Farm walks

Walk the rows by plan for larger orders, specimen selections, or material that should be seen before approval.

04

Pickup planning

Review timing, loading needs, and access details so crews can prepare before arriving at the farm.
