The Farm

A working grower, not a showroom.

Abel Tree Farm is a working tree farm in Loxahatchee, west of Palm Beach. We grow and source palms, coastal trees, privacy hedges, and field-grown nursery stock for South Florida landscapes.

Contractors, builders, estate managers, municipalities, and homeowners call before visiting so the farm can confirm what is available, what sizes are ready, and whether a farm walk makes sense.

Questions

Clear answers before the drive west.

Do you sell to landscapers and contractors?

Yes. Call or text with the plant name, size, quantity, project city, and timing so we can confirm current availability.

Can homeowners buy directly?

Yes, but please call first. Abel Tree Farm is a working farm, not a retail garden center. We can help confirm whether the material you need is available and whether a visit makes sense.

Is the inventory live?

No. Inventory changes by season, growth stage, and project volume. The website shows sample material. Call or text for current counts.

Do you handle permits or code approval?

No. Abel Tree Farm can help source plants suited to South Florida conditions. Landscape architects, contractors, and permitting professionals handle formal code compliance.
